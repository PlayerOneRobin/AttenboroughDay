A quiet invitation

One day each year to stop, look closer, and remember that wonder can become care.

International Attenborough Day is a global celebration of nature inspired by the life and work of Sir David Attenborough. On May 8, people everywhere are invited to reconnect with the wild, watch a nature documentary, step outside, and share their appreciation for the planet.

How to celebrate

Five ways to mark the day

Keep it simple. Choose one act of attention, one act of care, and one thing worth sharing.

01

Watch a nature documentary

Return to a film that makes the world feel larger and more alive.

02

Go outside and experience nature

Take a walk, notice the light, and listen for birds, wind, and water.

03

Plant something

Add one small sign of care to a garden, balcony, yard, or windowsill.

04

Share a photo of wildlife

Post a moment of wonder that helps someone else look more closely.

05

Teach someone about the planet

Pass on one fact, one story, or one reason to protect what is wild.
